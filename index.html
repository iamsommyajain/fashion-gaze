What is fashion trend forecasting?

Fashion trend forecasting utilizes data analysis, historical trends, and market research to predict future styles, colors, fabrics, and silhouettes. This helps brands, designers, and retailers anticipate what will be popular in upcoming seasons.

Mining the chicest data goldmines for you , Fashion Gaze analyses runway shows, street style snaps, influencer posts, historical trend archives, and sales charts to predict the next big thing in fashion.
